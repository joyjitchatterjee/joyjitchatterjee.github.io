About Dr. Chatterjee

Dr. Joyjit Chatterjee is a dedicated AI innovator and global data scientist, recognized in the Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe list for Manufacturing and Industry. His work focuses on developing AI solutions that aim to enhance energy efficiency and optimize manufacturing processes.

Currently serving as an IT & Digital Data Scientist - Continuous Improvement at Reckitt, a multinational FMCG company, Dr. Chatterjee contributes to supply chain optimization through applied AI solutions. His efforts are directed towards minimizing waste, optimizing quality, and enhancing productivity.

In his previous role as an R&D Data Scientist (KTP Associate) at Reckitt, Dr. Chatterjee developed AI models for shelf-life prediction and optimization of healthcare formulations. This project received the "Certificate of Excellence - Outstanding" from Innovate UK and was highlighted as a KTP success story by Ashorne Hill.

Dr. Chatterjee's expertise extends to the renewable energy sector, where he has contributed to data-driven operations and maintenance in the wind industry. His work in this area earned him the Green Talents Award from the German government and has been featured in global platforms such as the World Economic Forum and IEEE.

Recognized as a Global Talent by UKRI and the UK Home Office, Dr. Chatterjee offers voluntary AI advice to various sectors and has served as an expert reviewer on European Commission projects.

Dr. Chatterjee's academic journey includes a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Hull, UK, focusing on explainable AI for wind turbine maintenance. He also holds a PGDip in Research Training from Hull, a Professional Certificate in AI from Oxford, and a CPD Course in Business from Cambridge. His academic foundation was laid at Amity University, India, where he earned a Gold Medal in B.Tech Electronics & Communication Engineering.

As an active contributor to the academic community, Dr. Chatterjee has published in top-tier AI and engineering venues, including conferences like NeurIPS and KDD, and journals from Wiley, Elsevier, and Cambridge University Press. He has also filed patents and shared his insights through talks on global platforms.

Dr. Chatterjee's international experience includes roles as a Visiting Researcher at the University of Bremen, Germany, and Tamkang University, Taiwan. He regularly serves on committees for leading conferences and journals, bringing a global perspective to these academic forums.

A certified Scrum Master and avid globetrotter, Dr. Chatterjee continually seeks new challenges. His accolades include the Best International Knowledge Exchange Award, PhD Scholarship, Employability Award from Hull, grants from IEEE and Microsoft, and the Young Researcher Award from IEEE UK & Ireland.

Dr. Chatterjee's research interests span cutting-edge areas of AI, including Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Graphs, Causal Inference, and Time-Series Analysis. Through his work, he strives to contribute to innovation across industries and promote a more efficient, sustainable future.
