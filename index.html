About Me

As a Forbes 30 Under 30 Europe honoree in Manufacturing and Industry, I, Dr. Joyjit Chatterjee, have established myself as a pioneer in developing AI products that revolutionize energy and manufacturing processes. My journey in AI and data science spans multiple countries, sectors, and disciplines, making me a truly global innovator.

In this role, I innovate applied AI solutions for supply chain optimization, focusing on minimizing waste, optimizing quality, and enhancing productivity through advanced process optimization techniques.

This work has garnered attention from global platforms such as Forbes, the World Economic Forum, Times of India, and IEEE, underlining its significance in the field of sustainable energy.

Recognized as a Global Talent by UKRI and the UK Home Office, I frequently provide voluntary AI advice to C-Suite Executives, Civil Servants, and Academic Institutions. My global perspective is further enhanced by my experience as an expert reviewer on European Commission projects.

My academic journey is equally impressive. My educational background also includes a PGDip in Research Training from Hull, a Professional Certificate in AI from Oxford, and a CPD Course in Business from Cambridge. Beyond my technical expertise, I'm a certified Scrum Master and an avid globetrotter, continuously seeking new challenges and perspectives.
